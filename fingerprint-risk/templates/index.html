DEFENSE STRATEGIES
📸

PHOTO HYGIENE

  • Curl or fold fingers in photos
  • Keep hands away from camera
  • Use blur/mosaic on finger tips
  • Avoid high-res close-up hand shots
🔐

DEVICE SECURITY

  • Use PIN/password as primary lock
  • Enable liveness detection (if available)
  • Enable 2FA on all critical accounts
  • Avoid biometrics for banking apps
🛡️

AWARENESS

  • Educate friends & family
  • Review social media privacy settings
  • Monitor for account breaches
  • Report suspicious login attempts
